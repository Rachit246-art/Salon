[ About Us ]

Redefining Luxury in Bangalore

At Diorette Salon & Café, we’ve transformed the traditional salon experience into a luxurious lifestyle destination. Located in the heart of Bangalore, Diorette blends world-class beauty services with a premium café experience—allowing our guests to indulge in self-care, relaxation, and gourmet delights under one roof.

Our space is more than a salon; it’s a sanctuary where elegance, precision, and comfort converge to create unforgettable moments. With expert stylists, globally trusted beauty brands, and a meticulously curated café, Diorette sets a new benchmark in luxury grooming and lifestyle experiences.
