Sip, Relax & Indulge

The Diorette Café brings together luxury, leisure, and gourmet indulgence in a setting like no other. As India's first café integrated into a luxury salon, it offers a serene escape where beauty and lifestyle meet. Located in Bangalore, our café is designed for pause, pleasure, and self-care.

Why Visit Diorette Café?

1. Handcrafted Beverages

Expert baristas serve artisanal coffee and specialty drinks, prepared with care and precision to complement your salon experience.

2. Gourmet Bites

Enjoy a selection of pastries, light bites, and indulgent treats, crafted to elevate your moments of relaxation.

3. Aesthetic Ambiance

Our interiors are thoughtfully curated to create a luxurious, calming environment, perfect for unwinding before, during, or after styling.

4. Complete Luxury

At Diorette, café and salon blend seamlessly, making every visit an unforgettable journey of beauty, indulgence, and refined comfort.
